The IT sector is poised for robust growth, with worldwide IT spending projected to hit $5.61 trillion in 2025, a nearly 10% jump from 2024. Software is a key driver, expected to surge by 12% in 2025 and maintain over 10% annual growth through 2029. In the US, IT Services are set to generate over $563 billion next year, with outsourcing leading the charge. Globally, the IT Services market is on track to reach nearly $1.9 trillion by 2029. Fidelity Investments confirms the technology sector remains "well positioned," underscoring its vital role in shaping our future.
