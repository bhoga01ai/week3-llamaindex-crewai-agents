*   Worldwide IT spending is projected to reach $5.43 trillion in 2025, marking a 7.9% increase from 2024, according to Gartner.
*   Analysts anticipate global IT spending to grow by 9.3% in 2025, with the data center and software segments specifically expected to experience double-digit growth rates.
*   The IT Services market is forecasted to generate a substantial $1.50 trillion in revenue by 2025.
*   The software market is on track for robust growth, with projections indicating an annual increase of over 10% through 2029, which bodes well for its revenue outlook in 2025.
*   An earlier projection by Gartner, as cited by CompTIA, had estimated worldwide IT spending for 2025 at $5.75 trillion, suggesting a 9.3% growth over 2024.
